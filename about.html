About Me

Cybersecurity enthusiast and technology professional from New Jersey

Hello, I'm Aidan

I'm a senior honors student in the Information Technology Academy at Red Bank Regional High School. For over six years, I've been immersed in cybersecurity, competing in CyberPatriot since sixth grade and now serving as team captain, specializing in MS Windows Server 2022 and penetration testing.

Beyond the digital realm, I founded Lenahan Dog Services in 2021, building a successful business with over 40 clients through self-developed marketing and a custom website. Throughout high school, I have run varsity cross-country, track, and played club lacrosse. My hands-on experience spans network administration, low-voltage systems installation, and hardware refurbishment.
